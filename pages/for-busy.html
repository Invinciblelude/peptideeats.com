For Busy Professionals

You’re on calls all day, skip meals, slam coffee, and hit DoorDash at 9pm. Studies show long work hours cause real barriers to healthy eating—but simple planning can flip that completely.

The Data

Research shows 76% of professionals struggle with sustained healthy habits. Meal prep and routine win. 23% higher productivity and 40% less stress when eating patterns are consistent.

Time barriers

Long hours >40hr/week create real barriers. Staples—nuts, hard-boiled eggs, pre-cut veggies—crush the “no time” excuse.

80/20 rule

Home meals 80% of the time crush burnout. You don’t need perfection—you need consistency.

10-minute breakfasts

Protein + fiber (eggs + oats, or Greek yogurt + berries) within 2 hours of waking stabilizes energy and focus.
