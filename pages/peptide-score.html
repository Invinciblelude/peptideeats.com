PeptideEats Score: See Where You Really Stand

Your PeptideEats Score is a simple 0–100 snapshot of how your current eating patterns support long-term health habits. It is not a diagnosis — it is a starting point to see blind spots and your biggest opportunities.

How it works

  • Answer a short set of questions about how you actually eat in real life.
  • Make a suggested $10 donation to unlock your score and roadmap (this supports our tools — it is not a fee for medical care).
  • Receive your score and a simple written roadmap: your top food shifts to focus on first, plus protocol direction (typically within 24–48 hours by email).
  • Use it on your own timeline and bring it to your doctor if you choose. All content is self-guided.

Why we ask for a $10 suggested donation

We keep this almost free on purpose. A small donation is enough to show you are serious about investing in yourself — without letting money be the barrier.

Your support helps keep the PeptideEats Score online, improve it over time, and build new food-first protocols that stay accessible to regular people — not only clinic clients.

This is support for education, not a purchase of medical care. You are helping fund prevention-focused tools so more people can see where they stand and make smarter food decisions.

What you get

  • Your PeptideEats Score (0–100) based on your answers
  • A simple written roadmap with your top food shifts to focus on first
  • Protocol meal recommendations and starter plan direction by email
  • Optional reminders and education to help you act on your score

What it is not

  • Not diagnosis, treatment, or medical care
  • Not a replacement for your doctor or licensed clinician
  • Not individualized lab review or prescription advice

We do not review labs or provide individual medical advice at this tier. Talk with your licensed provider before changing medications or diet.
