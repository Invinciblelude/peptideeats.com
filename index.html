Why Your Routine Meals Matter More Than Your Injections

Peptides can be a reset button. Food is the operating system. PeptideEats shows you both—then keeps you off the yo-yo.

Peptide Power (Short-Term)

GLP-1 agonists help people lose 10–20% of body weight in trials. New triple agonists can reach ~24%. Great for fast risk reduction—especially in severe obesity.

Peptide Problems (Reality Check)

60–80% regain when stopped if habits don’t change. Up to 40% of weight lost can be lean muscle. Nausea, GI upset, fatigue lead many to quit. Experimental peptides lack solid human data and face FDA bans.

Food-First (Long-Term)

Healthy eating patterns cut death risk by ~20% and reduce heart disease, cancer, respiratory deaths. Legumes, whole grains, nuts can add 10–13 extra years. Routine meal timing improves weight, glucose, energy without drugs.
